Our Services

At Synergy Group, we offer a comprehensive range of maritime services tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. Our services are designed to ensure optimal efficiency, productivity, and safety for all types of vessels. Here is an overview of our core services:

Technical Management

We provide end-to-end technical management services for a diverse range of vessels, ensuring they operate efficiently and safely. Our technical management services include:

  • Regular maintenance and repairs
  • Dry docking supervision
  • Technical inspections and audits
  • Implementation of safety and environmental regulations

Crew Management

Our crew management services ensure that vessels are manned by highly skilled and experienced professionals. We handle all aspects of crew management, including:

  • Crew recruitment and training
  • Payroll and administration
  • Compliance with international maritime regulations
  • Health and safety management

Commercial Management

We offer commercial management services to maximize the profitability and operational efficiency of our clients' vessels. Our commercial management services include:

  • Chartering and brokerage
  • Voyage planning and optimization
  • Contract management
  • Market analysis and reporting

New Building Supervision

Our new building supervision services ensure that new vessels are built to the highest standards and meet all regulatory requirements. Our services include:

  • Project management and planning
  • Construction supervision
  • Quality control and assurance
  • Commissioning and delivery

Conversions and Upgrades

We provide comprehensive conversion and upgrade services to enhance the performance and capabilities of existing vessels. Our services include:

  • Feasibility studies and project planning
  • Engineering and design
  • Construction and installation
  • Testing and commissioning

Recycling and Decommissioning

We offer environmentally responsible recycling and decommissioning services for end-of-life vessels. Our services include:

  • Decommissioning planning and management
  • Compliance with environmental regulations
  • Safe dismantling and recycling
  • Waste management and disposal

Maritime Consultancy

Our maritime consultancy services provide expert advice and support to help our clients navigate complex maritime challenges. Our consultancy services include:

  • Regulatory compliance
  • Risk management
  • Operational efficiency improvements
  • Strategic planning and development

At Synergy Group, we are committed to delivering top-quality maritime services that meet the unique needs of our clients. Contact us to learn more about how we can support your maritime operations.
